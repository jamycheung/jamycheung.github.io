Jiaming Zhang

PhD Student

KIT, Germany

       

I am a PhD student at the Computer Vision for Human-Computer Interaction (CV:HCI) Lab at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Germany, under the supervision of Prof. Rainer Stiefelhagen and Dr. Kailun Yang. I received my B.Sc degree in CSSE from Shenzhen University (SZU). I received my M.Sc degree in Informatics from KIT.

My research interest lie in the combination of computer vision and assistive technology, and building human-friendly systems for people with visual impairments. The topics included are scene understanding, visual localization, and their applications in assistive system, human-computer interaction and intelligent vehicles.

Looking for a bachelor/master thesis? Please send me an email if you are interested in any of the topics below.
